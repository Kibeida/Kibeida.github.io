History of experience

My most recent role was at Amazon Web Services (AWS) in Dublin, Ireland. I worked as a Graduate Cloud Support Associate as part of a graduate program. I joined the program shortly after completing a computer science master in University College Cork in Cork, Ireland. I also spent a short period working for Globetech in Cork, working on Linguistic Analysis on Apple’s Siri. Before moving to Ireland to pursue my Masters, I spent a few years in Dubai UAE working for dnata, a global ground handling service provider where I was a Ramp Operations Team Leader. In Sudan, my last role was a one year project working as an Interpreter/Translator contractor for the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA). Before that, and for over two years, I was an IT Assistant working for the United Nations Mission in Sudan, providing technical support for the UN Logistic Base in North Kordofan.
